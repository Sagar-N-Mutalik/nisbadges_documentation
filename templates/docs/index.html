About

What is NISBadges?

NISBadges is an initiative to recognize and encourage members who consistently attend events, contribute meaningfully, and have an impactful presence within the IEEE community. It rewards holistic involvement rather than just raw attendance numbers. By celebrating every dimension of participation — from showing up to leading, from ideating to executing — NISBadges ensures that the right people are seen, celebrated, and empowered to grow into the leaders of tomorrow.

Purpose

Why It Matters

NISBadges provides tangible recognition and opens real doors for dedicated IEEE members.

Credibility

Earn pride and visibility within the IEEE community for your consistent, meaningful contributions and show the world what you stand for.

Recognition

Be identified as a future leader. NISBadges highlights your commitment to excellence and amplifies your voice within the branch.

Opportunities

Receive priority consideration for core team positions and ExeCom leadership roles — because your impact earns you access.

Reward

Take home tangible proof of your dedication. A NISBadge is a permanent record of the impact you have made in the community.

Recognition Levels

The Three Tiers

Three distinct levels of recognition, each honouring a different degree of commitment, contribution, and impact.

Tier III
PRO
Top 20 Members

Recognizes members who are constantly present and engaged at IEEE events. PRO badge holders embody consistency — their commitment to showing up and staying active is the foundation every great branch is built on. This badge encourages them to push for active ExeCom roles and step into greater leadership.

Tier II
ELITE
Top 10 Members

Awarded to proactive volunteers who contribute significantly during the planning and execution of events. ELITE members go beyond attendance — they roll up their sleeves, take ownership, and actively shape the branch's initiatives. Their effort drives the events that everyone remembers.

Tier I
PRIME
Top 5 Members

The highest distinction in NISBadges. Reserved for enthusiastic members who contribute across every facet — from ideation to promotion, from planning to impactful execution and measurable results. PRIME represents excellence in all dimensions and is a mark of true community leadership.

Process

How It Works

A transparent, merit-based process that rewards real involvement at every step.

1

Participate & Contribute

Attend IEEE events, volunteer for planning sessions, take on tasks, and contribute across ideation, execution, and promotion throughout the semester.

2

Score & Evaluate

A holistic scoring system measures attendance, volunteering depth, role contributions, and overall impact — going beyond just showing up.

3

Earn Your Badge

Top performers are awarded their NISBadge tier at an official recognition event — a milestone in your IEEE journey and your professional identity.
