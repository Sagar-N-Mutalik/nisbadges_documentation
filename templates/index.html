What is NISBadges?

NISBadges is an initiative to recognize and encourage members who consistently attend events, contribute meaningfully, and have an impactful presence within the IEEE community. It rewards holistic involvement rather than just raw attendance numbers.

Why It Matters

NISBadges provides tangible recognition and opportunities for dedicated IEEE members

Credibility

Gain pride and visibility within the IEEE community for your consistent contributions.

Recognition

Identify future leaders and showcase your commitment to excellence.

Opportunities

Receive priority consideration for core team positions and leadership roles.

Reward

Earn tangible proof of your impact and dedication to the community.

The Three Tiers

Recognition levels designed to honor different degrees of commitment and impact

Tier III

PRO
Top 20 Members

Recognizes members who are constantly present, encouraging them to push for active ExeCom roles. Consistent attendance and engagement are the hallmarks of PRO badge holders.

Tier II

ELITE
Top 10 Members

Awarded to proactive volunteers who contribute significantly during the planning and execution of events. ELITE members go beyond attendance to actively shape our initiatives.

Tier I

PRIME
Top 5 Members

The highest distinction. For enthusiastic members contributing across multiple facets, from ideation to promotion and impactful results. PRIME represents excellence in all dimensions.
